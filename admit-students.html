Admission Best Practices

Keep each application complete to speed admin review and place students in the right learning track.

  • Use the full legal name and correct email address.
  • Select the student’s grade/class accurately.
  • Choose at least three subjects to reflect academic interests.
  • Provide a clear school name and pathway choice.

Quick note
