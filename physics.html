Chapter 1: Mechanics

Mechanics is the branch of physics concerned with the motion of physical objects.

It includes kinematics (study of motion) and dynamics (study of forces causing motion).

Fundamental principles:

  • Newton's Laws of Motion
  • Conservation of Energy
  • Conservation of Momentum
  • Gravitational Force

Key Notes

F = ma (Newton's second law): Force equals mass times acceleration.

Energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed (First Law of Thermodynamics).
